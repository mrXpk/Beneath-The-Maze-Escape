🎮 3D Maze Escape

Find the exit. Stay calm. Don’t get lost.

3D Maze Escape is an immersive first-person 3D maze adventure where players must navigate through a complex labyrinth and find the way out. With smooth controls and atmospheric design, the game challenges your sense of direction and focus.

🧠 About The Game

You wake up inside a mysterious maze.

Tall walls surround you.
Every path looks the same.
Some routes lead nowhere.

Your mission is simple:
Find the exit and escape.

No map.
No hints.
Just your instincts.
